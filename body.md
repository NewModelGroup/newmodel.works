New Model Works is a software lab that builds modern, efficient, opinionated tools for academics.
It is part of the [New Model Group](https://newmodel.group).

The lab seeks to demonstrate that modern computing can and must be efficient in its use of energy and water, and in its impact on attention and mental health.
The lab acknowledges that artificial intelligence has a clear role for academics, particularly those working in the social sciences.
However, we only use large-language models that have been trained on open-source datasets; we actively avoid using ethically problematic data sources.
All our LLMs are minimal and stripped-down, and where possible run at the 'edge' i.e. directly on your computer.
All our hosting is powered by 100% renewable energy sources.
We are also investigating how to mitigate our water impact and how best to support causes such as those protecting water access and water purification.
Once this is clear we will publish our methodology for others to copy and critique.
